General Information

The Rixy Club is specially designed for children. Our younger guests will feel at home in a world of imagination and fun. Our friendly proficient team is dedicated to providing memorable moments for the kids, through educational entertainment programs tailored to different ages, interests, and eneray levels. Little quests will have hours of fun participating in water games, taking part in evening shows, producing works of art, and doing competitions. They will probably never want to go home!
