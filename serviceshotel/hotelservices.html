Adaptors

We are happy to provide you with a power adaptor. Please contact reception (5000).

ATM

An ATM (Kapital Bank) is located on the L floar near the shops.

Bathrobes

You can find bathrobes in your bathroom. Please contact the reception if you need additional bathrobes.

Blanket, Pillow & Coat hangers

You can find blankets and pillows in your wardrobe. We can provide additional blankets and pillows on request. Coat hangers are located in the wardrobe. If you require more blankets, pillows, or coat hangers please contact the reception.

Breakfast

Breakfast is served in -1 floor, Mosaic Restaurant from 7am to 11am.

Business Center

For business purposes, we can provide you with a personal computer and free WIFI connection.

Butler Service

Butler Service is available 24 hours a day. Please call the reception. Fees apply for Butler Services.

Cinema Club

You can enjoy different movies and cartoons every day in our Cinema Club, located on the M floor (elevator beside SPA reception). Entrance is free for our guests.

Fax, Photocopy and Scaning Service

These services can be requested from the reception and are free of charge.

Concierge Service

Information on the many attractions of the Quba region is available from our Concierge. Concierge Service is available 24 hours a day and is located next to the reception. Our staff are always approachable happy to provide you with any directions you may require. We can arrange transportation for you. Please notify us with your request one day prior. Helicopter arrangements are available. Please notify us with your request 5-4 days prior. We are also happy to provide you with information on the local area.

Doctor Service & Pharmacy

Doctor service is available from 11am to 5pm. Pharmacy service is available 24 hours. For medical requests, please contact the reception (5000).

Extra bed, baby cot and rollaway beds

Extra bed service is available and is chargeable. Baby cots are available free of charge.
