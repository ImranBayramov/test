Fire Alarm

The hotel emergency hotline is: 5000 Our Security is available to help you 24 hours a day. When you arrive, we recommend that you take a few precautionary steps: Locate emergency exits by reviewing the map on the back of your guest room door. Locate the nearest fire extinguisher and fire alarms. Hotel Assembly point is the outdoor pool area, Our hotel is equipped with a modern , efhcient, fire emergency system, which includes a sprinkler system and smoke detectors in guest rooms and hallways. If any of our detection systems sounds a warning, please call the Operator by dialing 5000. If you must leave your room, feel the door to check if it is hot. If the door is hot, open the door slightly to see if you can exit safely to the nearest evacuation staircase. Stay low to the floor and do not forget to take your room key. Do not use the elevator. If it is too hot, stay in your room and seal your door with wet towels. Turn off your air conditioner or heater to keep smoke from entering the room. Get down on the floor to avoid inhaling smoke. For your own safety please note the location of each exit upon arrival.
